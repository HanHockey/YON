About Us

We came up with the idea of creating this Yes Or No generator because we found that there is no website where you can input a question and just get a yes or no answer. If you search a yes or no answer on a web browser, a bunch of links show up and you have to click on a link and search through the website to find a direct yes or no answer. With this website, you can input any question, and a simple yes or no will come up on the screen. We hope this helps and makes it easier to find the answer to a yes or no question.
